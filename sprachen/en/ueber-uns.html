About this project

Website last checked: 12 July 2026 · updated: 12 July 2026

What is Digitale Ersthilfe?

Digitale Ersthilfe is a website with simple guides for people who face digital violence. This can be, for example:

  • Surveillance through devices or software (stalkerware, smart-home devices)
  • Hacked accounts (email, social networks, online banking)
  • Harassment, threats or hate on the internet
  • Phishing, scams and identity theft

Our guides explain in simple language what affected people can do, step by step. Every text can also be read aloud.

Who is behind it?

This service is run by Novum Analytica GmbH. Novum Analytica takes part in the voluntary Cyber Security Network (CSN) of the German Federal Office for Information Security (BSI) — but is not part of the BSI and does not act on its behalf.

As digital first responders and CSN practitioners, we create practical materials for affected people and for other helpers.

Who is this page for?

  • Affected people (non-experts): People who face digital violence and need quick, easy-to-understand help.
  • Digital first responders: Volunteers and professionals who help affected people on site.
  • CSN practitioners: Experts in the BSI's Cyber Security Network.
  • Incident experts: Specialists who advise on IT security incidents.

Our principles

  • Simple language: All texts are written so that you can understand them without any special knowledge.
  • Accessibility: The page is built so that as many people as possible can use it — no matter what limitations they have. Text size and contrast can be adjusted. All content can be read aloud.
  • Focus on action: We do not just tell you what the problem is, but what you can do right now.
  • Works offline: You can also use this page without the internet, once you have saved it on your device (as an app).

Important notes

Independent, voluntary service

This website is a voluntary service by Novum Analytica. It is not provided, funded or run by the German Federal Office for Information Security (BSI). There is no official connection with the BSI.

Not legal advice

All content is for first information only. It does not replace professional legal advice, IT forensics, psychological care or a police investigation. You use all content at your own responsibility.

Note on AI support and authorship

All content is checked and improved for readability, wording and clarity with the help of AI-based software. Images and infographics may be AI-generated or AI-edited.

Even so: every piece of content is originally written by human authors. In the end, all texts are always reviewed and approved by human editors.
