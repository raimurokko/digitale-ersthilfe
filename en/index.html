Quick help with digital problems

Did someone steal your password? Are you being harassed online? Is your device acting strangely? Here you will find simple steps you can take right now.

All texts are written in simple, easy-to-read language. You can also have any text read aloud to you.

Browse without being watched

Are you safe? Tips for browsing without being watched
  • If you can, use a device that the person cannot access — the person you want to protect yourself from. For example, the phone of someone you trust, or a computer at the library.
  • Open a private (incognito) window: Press Ctrl + Shift + N and open this page there again. This leaves fewer traces on the device.
  • Delete your history: Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete to open your browser's delete window. Please note: if your whole history is suddenly gone, that can also look suspicious — if in doubt, delete only the "last hour".
  • This website itself does not store anything about you — no cookies, no tracking. But the visit still stays in your browser history until you delete it.
  • With the "Quick exit" button (bottom left) or by pressing Esc twice, you switch to a neutral page at once.
