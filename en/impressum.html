Principle: Minimal data collection

This website collects as little personal data as possible. We use no tracking cookies, no analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) and no advertising.

Hosting and server logs

When you visit this website, the hosting provider automatically collects technical data (so-called server log files). This includes:

  • IP address (anonymised)
  • Date and time of access
  • Page requested
  • Browser and operating system

This data is not merged with other data sources and is deleted automatically after 7 days. The legal basis is Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. f DSGVO (legitimate interest in the technical provision of the website).

Fonts (hosted locally)

All fonts on this website are served locally from our own server. There is no connection to Google Fonts or other external providers. When the fonts load, no data is sent to third parties and your IP address is not passed on to external font servers.

Progressive Web App (PWA)

This website can be installed as an app on your device. Content is then stored locally on your device, so you can use the page even without an internet connection. In doing so, no personal data is sent to us.

Read-aloud function

The read-aloud function uses your browser's speech synthesis (Web Speech API). The texts are processed locally on your device and are not sent to external servers.
