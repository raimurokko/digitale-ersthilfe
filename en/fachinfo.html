Technical details for professionals

  • MAC addresses of Amazon Echo: Start with 44:65:0D or 68:37:E9.
  • Router logs (FritzBox): Can be exported under "System" → "Events" → "Internet connection". Document suspicious connections to Amazon servers outside active use times.
  • Drop In function: Allows real-time listening in with no visible sign on the device. Can be set up in the Alexa app under "Communication" → "Drop In".
  • DSGVO data request: Affected people can request a copy of their data at amazon.de/privacy — but this only covers their own account.
  • Legal basis: § 201 StGB — violation of the confidentiality of the spoken word. Penalty of up to 3 years in prison. The police can require Amazon to hand over activity data and voice recordings.

Technical details for professionals

  • Attack mechanism: Misuse of the normal function "Linked Devices" (Signal) or "Companion Mode" (WhatsApp). The victim scans a QR code created by the attacker — the attacker's device gets access to new incoming and outgoing messages in real time.
  • Social engineering vectors: Fake Signal/WhatsApp notifications, fake group invitations ("Secure Chat"), compromised accounts of known contacts, time pressure and pretending to be an authority.
  • Attribution: Google TAG / Microsoft Threat Intelligence report on Star Blizzard (Coldriver, UNC4057) — an FSB-affiliated Russian actor. The technique is also copied by non-state groups.
  • Detection indicators on end devices: Unknown sessions under Settings → Linked / Companion devices. In Signal also: a new "safety number" for contacts.
  • Countermeasures at the organisation level: Mobile Device Management (MDM) with policies against scanning QR codes from external sources, awareness training, replacing Signal/WhatsApp with an enterprise messenger that has device attestation (e.g. Matrix / Element with SSO).
  • Legal basis: § 202a StGB (data espionage) and § 263a StGB (computer fraud). Where there is an intelligence-service background, also §§ 99, 100 StGB (agent activity for a foreign secret service).

To the guide for non-experts (simple language)

Confidential securing of evidence lets victims of violence get timely medical documentation and evidence collection without involving the police. The samples are stored in a legally secure way; the affected person decides later whether to report the crime.

Legal basis

  • SGB V § 27 Abs. 1a (since 01.03.2020): In some federal states, the health insurers cover the costs.
  • Istanbul Convention (Art. 25): An obligation to provide low-barrier protection and support services for victims of sexual violence.
